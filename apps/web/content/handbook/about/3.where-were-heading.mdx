Char starts as a notepad. It ends as a chief of staff.

## Step 1: The meeting notepad

Char captures every conversation — yours first, then your team's, then your organization's. It starts with one person who loves it and spreads from there. Over time, Char becomes the source of truth for everything discussed across the company. Not recordings collecting dust — living, searchable context.

## Step 2: Action items for everyone

Meetings produce action items. Char derives them automatically — not just for you, but for every person in the organization. Individual to-dos roll up into a comprehensive picture of what needs to happen across the team. A checklist that writes itself.

## Step 3: Orchestration

Once Char knows what needs to be done and who owns it, it can start doing the work. Integrations with GitHub, Slack, email, and the tools your team already uses mean Char can act — create a PR, send a follow-up, file an issue — without you context-switching. The action item doesn't just sit on a list. It moves.

## The end state

Char becomes an AI chief of staff. Not a project management tool — something closer to the person who sits next to the CEO and makes sure everything actually happens. It lives in Slack, your email, everywhere. It has full context — every meeting, every decision, every commitment — so it orchestrates work across the team, removes blockers, and acts on your behalf. The operational overhead that slows teams down disappears.
